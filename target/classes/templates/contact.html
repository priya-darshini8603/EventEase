Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first event on EventEase?
Creating your first event is simple! After registering and logging in, click on "Create Event" in your dashboard. Fill in the event details like name, date, location, and description. You can make it public or private, set ticket prices, and customize your event page.
Is EventEase free to use?
Yes! EventEase offers a free plan that includes basic event creation and management features. We also offer premium plans with advanced features like detailed analytics, custom branding, and priority support.
Can I sell tickets through EventEase?
Absolutely! EventEase supports both free and paid events. You can set ticket prices, manage inventory, and process payments securely through our integrated payment system.
How do I promote my event?
EventEase provides several promotion tools including shareable event pages, social media integration, email marketing features, and the ability to list your event in our public directory for maximum visibility.
What kind of support do you offer?
We offer email support for all users, with response times typically within 24 hours. Premium users get priority support with faster response times and phone support options.
